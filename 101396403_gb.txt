George Brown College (GBC) is a renowned post-secondary institution located in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. Established in 1967, GBC offers a diverse range of programs spanning various fields of study, including business, hospitality, health sciences, community services, arts, design, technology, and construction.

Key highlights of George Brown College include:

Programs and Courses: GBC offers over 160 programs and 1,600 courses across its three campuses: St. James Campus, Casa Loma Campus, and Waterfront Campus. These programs range from certificates and diplomas to degrees and post-graduate certificates.

Practical Education: GBC emphasizes hands-on, experiential learning, providing students with real-world skills and knowledge that are directly applicable to their chosen careers.

Industry Partnerships: The college has strong ties with industry partners, enabling students to gain valuable work experience through internships, co-op placements, and applied research projects.

Diversity and Inclusion: GBC is committed to fostering a diverse and inclusive learning environment where students from all backgrounds feel welcome and supported.

State-of-the-Art Facilities: George Brown College boasts modern facilities equipped with cutting-edge technology, including specialized labs, studios, and simulation centers.
